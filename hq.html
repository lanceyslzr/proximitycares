⬡ Operator Guide 12 SOPs ›
01
Add a new client
Go to Onboard tab → Fill in client name, address, care notes, medications, family email → Generate PIN → Send intake link. Client appears in Clients tab within 60 seconds.
02
Add a new PSW
Go to Onboard tab → Enter PSW name, email, hourly rate → Assign to client → Generate PIN → PSW installs portal at proximitycares.ca/psw and enters their PIN.
03
Schedule a shift
Go to Schedule tab → Drag the time slot to assign a PSW to a client → Set duration → Save. PSW gets a notification automatically.
04
Broadcast an open shift
Go to Schedule tab → Tap "Broadcast Open Shift" → Select client, date, time → Send. All active PSWs get a notification. First to accept is confirmed. No screenshots, no email threads.
05
Handle a PSW cancellation request
When a PSW taps "Cannot Make This Shift" you see it in the Today tab under Cancellation Requests. Tap Approve to auto-broadcast the shift, or Reject to keep the shift assigned.
06
Read the health console
Visit proximitysystems.ca/health. Green = OK, Amber = Warning (non-urgent), Red = Critical (needs attention). 0 critical means your platform is fully operational.
07
Handle a GPS exception
Go to Overview tab → GPS Exceptions panel. Out-of-range means PSW checked in more than 300m from the client address. Tap Map to see where they actually were. Review with PSW.
08
Resolve an open incident
Go to Visits tab → Filter by Incidents → Tap the visit → Tap Resolve Incident → Add resolution note. Family is NOT automatically notified — contact them directly for serious incidents.
09
Read an AI care summary
Go to Visits tab → Tap any completed visit → Expand. The AI summary is generated automatically at checkout from the PSW's voice note and checklist. It describes what was done and any flags.
10
Generate an invoice
Go to Revenue tab → Invoices auto-generate on the 1st of each month. To generate manually tap "Generate Invoice" on a client card. Mark paid when received.
11
Handle a PSW no-show
If a PSW misses a shift the no-show check fires automatically within 15 minutes. You get an alert. Go to Today tab → find the shift → tap Message PSW → if no response use Broadcast Open Shift to find coverage.
12
Kill a PSW session remotely
If a PSW loses their phone go to Overview tab → Active Sessions panel → find the PSW → tap Kill Session. Their portal will lock immediately and require re-PIN on next open.
PSW Guide 8 SOPs ›
01
Install the PSW portal
iPhone: Open Safari → go to proximitycares.ca/psw → tap the Share button → "Add to Home Screen" → Done. Android: Open Chrome → go to the URL → tap the 3 dots → "Add to Home Screen".
02
First login
Open the app → enter your 4-digit PIN → your schedule loads automatically. If you forget your PIN contact your coordinator.
03
Check in to a visit
From the Today tab tap the next visit card → tap Sign In. Allow location access when prompted. GPS is captured once at checkin — this verifies you are at the client address.
04
Check out of a visit
Tap Check Out → complete the mood selector, task checklist, MAR medications → record a 30-second voice note describing the visit → tap Complete Checkout. AI summary is generated automatically.
05
Emergency during a visit
Tap the SOS button on the active visit card → all emergency contacts appear with tap-to-call buttons. For life-threatening emergencies call 911 first, then notify your coordinator.
06
No internet during a shift
The portal works offline. Checkin and checkout actions queue locally and sync automatically when you reconnect. The sync status in the drawer shows when data was last synced.
07
Accept an open shift
Open the Schedule tab → scroll down to Open Shifts Available → tap Accept on any shift you can cover. Your coordinator is notified immediately. The shift appears on your schedule.
08
Cannot make a shift
On the shift card in Schedule tap "Cannot Make This Shift" → select a reason → Send Request. Your shift stays assigned until your coordinator approves. Do NOT assume the shift is cancelled.
Compliance 5 SOPs ›
01
Ontario documentation requirements
Every visit requires: PSW name, client name, time in, time out, tasks completed, client mood, medications given, and an AI-generated care summary. Incidents must be documented at checkout.
02
Refusal of care protocol
If a client refuses care: use the GPA approach (Gentle, Persuade, Allow to settle). If they still refuse, log it at checkout using the Refusal of Care checkboxes. Notify family. Document everything.
03
Incident escalation
HIGH severity (fall, injury, medical emergency, elopement): call 911 first, then coordinator, then family. Document in checkout incident report. LOW severity (minor behavioral, environmental): document at checkout, notify coordinator within 2 hours.
04
MAR requirements
Every medication administered must be logged at checkout under Medications Given. Include medication name and time given. Missed medications must be noted in observations.
05
Care plan acknowledgement
PSWs must acknowledge the care plan before their first visit with each client every 30 days. This is prompted automatically in the portal. The acknowledgement is logged with timestamp.
