Service Agreement — Proximity Care Services Inc.

This agreement is between Proximity Care Services Inc. ("the Agency"), a private home care agency operating in the Peel Region of Ontario, and the undersigned family representative ("the Client Family") on behalf of the care recipient named in this intake form.

1. Services. Proximity Care Services Inc. agrees to provide personal support worker (PSW) services as described in the care plan established during the intake process. Services may include personal care, meal preparation, companionship, light housekeeping, mobility assistance, and medication reminders (reminders only — PSWs do not administer medications).

2. Rates and Billing. Services are billed at the hourly rate agreed upon at the time of scheduling. Invoices are issued monthly and are due within 14 days of issue. A late payment fee of 1.5% per month may apply to overdue balances. Rates are subject to change with 30 days written notice.

3. Scheduling. The Agency will make reasonable efforts to provide consistent PSW coverage. In cases of PSW illness, emergency, or unavailability, the Agency will arrange alternate coverage where possible. The Client Family agrees to provide at least 24 hours notice to cancel or reschedule a visit. Cancellations with less than 24 hours notice may be charged at 50% of the scheduled visit rate.

4. PSW Conduct. All Proximity Care PSWs have undergone a criminal background check, hold a valid PSW certificate, and maintain current CPR and First Aid certification. PSWs are employed by the Agency and are not independent contractors. The Client Family agrees not to directly hire any Proximity Care PSW for a period of 12 months following the end of services.

5. Termination. Either party may terminate this agreement with 7 days written notice. The Agency reserves the right to terminate services immediately in cases of unsafe working conditions, abuse, or failure to pay outstanding invoices.

6. Confidentiality and Privacy. All client and family information collected during intake and throughout service delivery is kept confidential and handled in accordance with Ontario's Personal Health Information Protection Act (PHIPA). Information is not shared with third parties except as required by law or with the explicit consent of the Client Family.

7. Liability. Proximity Care Services Inc. carries general liability insurance. The Agency is not responsible for pre-existing medical conditions or accidents resulting from the care recipient's own actions. The Client Family agrees to disclose all known medical conditions, medications, and risks prior to service commencement.

8. Governing Law. This agreement is governed by the laws of the Province of Ontario.
